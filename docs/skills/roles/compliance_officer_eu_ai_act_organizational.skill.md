# Role Skill: Compliance Officer (EU AI Act, Organizational)

## Mission of the role
Define governance, accountability, and human oversight structures required for EU AI Act compliance.

## Responsibilities
- Establish governance and accountability structures.
- Define human oversight requirements.
- Specify documentation and audit evidence needs.
- Align organizational controls to EU AI Act obligations.

## Knowledge Base interaction (read/write duties)
- Read: `docs/knowledge_base/eu_ai_act_mapping.md` and `docs/knowledge_base/lessons_learned.md`.
- Write: Maintain compliance evidence structure in `docs/knowledge_base/lessons_learned.md`.
- Verify: Governance artifacts align with Knowledge Base mappings.

## Inputs and Outputs (artifacts)
Inputs:
- Requirements and system context.
- Organizational policies and constraints.
- EU AI Act references.
Outputs:
- Governance and oversight guidelines.
- Documentation and audit evidence lists.
- Organizational control mappings.

## Definition of Done
- Governance roles and accountability are defined.
- Human oversight requirements are explicit.
- Documentation and audit evidence are specified.
- Organizational controls map to EU AI Act obligations.

## Typical Failure Modes
- Governance roles unclear or missing.
- Oversight requirements not actionable.
- Evidence expectations not defined.
- Control mappings lack traceability.

## Handoff rules to other roles
- Provide governance requirements with references.
- Flag gaps in organizational readiness.
- Coordinate with technical compliance controls.

## Purpose
Map gateway controls and documentation artifacts to EU AI Act obligations at a high level to support compliance readiness.

- Risk management system: Linked to FR-3, FR-5, NFR-O2.
- Data governance and protection: Linked to FR-4, NFR-S1.
- Cybersecurity and robustness: Linked to FR-3, FR-5, NFR-S2.
- Logging and record-keeping: Linked to FR-6, NFR-C1, NFR-C2.
- Human oversight and governance: Linked to NFR-O3, NFR-C2.

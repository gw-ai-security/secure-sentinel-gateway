# Portfolio Storyline - Compliance Readiness

## Situation
Regulatory pressure, including EU AI Act obligations, required clear technical and organizational safeguards for AI usage.

## Task
Translate compliance expectations into actionable, documentation-first controls for the gateway.

## Action
Mapped requirements to high-level EU AI Act obligations, defined audit evidence artifacts, and documented governance and oversight needs.

## Result
Produced audit-ready documentation that aligns security, logging, and governance with compliance expectations.

## Learning
Compliance is strongest when evidence artifacts are defined early and tied to requirements.

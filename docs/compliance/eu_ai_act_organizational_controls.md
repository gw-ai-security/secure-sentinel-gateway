# EU AI Act Organizational Controls - High-Level Mapping

## Purpose
Define governance, accountability, and oversight structures required to support EU AI Act compliance for the Secure Sentinel AI Gateway.

## Organizational control mappings (high-level)
| Control area | Description | Related requirements | EU AI Act obligation (high-level) | Evidence artifacts |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Governance and accountability | Assign ownership for AI risk, security, and compliance | NFR-C3, NFR-O3 | Governance and accountability | RACI notes, role definitions |
| Human oversight | Establish review and escalation paths for AI incidents | NFR-O2, NFR-C2 | Human oversight and intervention | Oversight policy, escalation procedures |
| Documentation and auditability | Maintain documentation and evidence for audits | NFR-C1, NFR-C2 | Technical documentation and record-keeping | Audit evidence register, compliance summaries |
| Change management | Track and approve policy and control changes | NFR-O3 | Lifecycle management and change control | Change approval logs |

## Notes
- These controls describe organizational safeguards; implementation details are intentionally excluded.
- Evidence artifacts listed here should be created and maintained per organizational policy.
